An endless to-do list but not enough gratification?




If so, you've come to the right place.


This is an app I designed to help make accountability easier to exercise,
for ourselves and for the people we care about.

It goes like this.


You enter a list of tasks that you'd like to get done but find difficult to get started or finish off for good.
You find friends who either have their own list of tasks or are otherwise very invested in your self-development.
Both of you make an account on the app and partner up.

Once you choose to start a task, the clock starts ticking.
The status and start time of your tasks are in full view only to those you've partnered up with.



After you've completed your task, you can set it to "Completed",
but it will still need to be accepted by one of your partners
for your achievements to go into your wallet.
---maybe have a way for partners to edit allocated time before accepting--

What is your wallet? It's a virtual account that calculates the time you put
into your tasks with the minimum wage in your area.

While this isn't linked to any real finances *yet*, it's a quick measure of how much you've achieved in total.
This way, you can see if you really can indulge in that guilty pleasure purchase every now and then
(with a little less guilt) or work yourself up into doing so!

If you've made a guilty pleasure purchase, you can also take that amount out of this virtual wallet.
This would set your wallet back and help you conceptualize how much you'll need to work on
your personal task list before going off on another shopping spree.

Like the name suggests, this is an honor system.
No one but your partners will come after you if you fudge the time every now and then.
(And if they're good partners, they might!)

But it's your wallet, not ours.
